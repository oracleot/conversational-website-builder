## The Guided Conversation

### Step 1: Business Type (üè¢)

The AI will ask you to describe your business. Simply explain what you do in natural language.

**Example responses:**
- "I run a management consulting firm that helps small businesses grow"
- "I own a local bakery specializing in custom wedding cakes"
- "We're a digital marketing agency focused on e-commerce brands"

The AI will identify whether you're a:
- **Service Business** (consulting, agencies, professional services)
- **Local Business** (restaurants, salons, retail stores)

### Step 2: Business Info (üìù)

Share your:
- Business name
- Tagline or slogan
- Target audience
- Brand personality (e.g., "professional but friendly", "modern and innovative")

**Example:**
> "My firm is called 'Strategic Growth Partners' with the tagline 'Empowering Small Businesses to Achieve Big Results'. We're professional but approachable."

### Step 3-9: Section Content

For each website section, the AI will ask targeted questions:

| Section | What You'll Provide |
|---------|---------------------|
| Hero | Main headline, call-to-action text |
| Services | List of services with descriptions |
| About | Your story, mission, team info |
| Process | How you work with clients |
| Portfolio | Past projects or case studies |
| Testimonials | Client quotes and reviews |
| Contact | Email, phone, location |

### Step 10: Review (‚úÖ)

## Troubleshooting

### The AI Didn't Understand My Response
Try rephrasing with more context:
- Original: "Web design"
- Better: "I offer web design services including custom websites, landing pages, and e-commerce stores"

### I Want to Change a Previous Answer
Currently, you can refresh the page and your conversation history will be preserved. Full section editing is coming in a future update.

### The Preview Isn't Updating
The preview updates after completing the Business Info step (Step 2). Keep answering questions to see your website build in real-time.

### Page Shows an Error
- Refresh the page‚Äîyour conversation is automatically saved
- Check your internet connection
- Contact support if the issue persists

## AI-Powered Design Selection (Phase 5)

### How It Works

The AI analyzes your **brand personality** to automatically select the best design variant for each section. There are 5 variants per section:

| Variant | Style | Best For |
|---------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Professional | Corporate, consulting, law firms |
| 2 | Modern | Tech startups, agencies, SaaS |
| 3 | Bold | Creative agencies, artists, designers |
| 4 | Elegant | Luxury brands, high-end services, boutiques |
| 5 | Friendly | Local businesses, healthcare, education |

### Viewing AI Recommendations

When you hover over any section in the preview, you'll see:
- **AI Badge**: Shows why the AI selected this variant based on your brand
- **"See other options" Button**: Opens the variant carousel

### Switching Variants

1. Hover over any section in the preview
2. Click **"See other options"**
3. Browse all 5 variants with match scores
4. Click on a variant to switch instantly
5. Use **‚Üê ‚Üí** arrow keys to navigate
6. Press **Esc** to close the carousel

### Understanding Match Scores

Each variant shows a **match percentage** based on your brand personality:
- **70%+** (Green): Excellent match
- **40-69%** (Yellow): Good match
- **<40%** (Gray): May work but less aligned

### AI Reasoning

The AI explains its selection, for example:
> "Selected variant 4 (85% match) because your 'elegant, sophisticated' brand personality aligns with its premium and refined design style."
